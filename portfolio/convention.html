AIA Convention 2016 - Imagine+

curating / exhibit design / illustration

Imagine+ is about making the ordinary extraordinary. It's about realizing architects endless potential to drive real change and add value for clients, projects, and communities.

The Imagine+ gallery showcased AIA's 2016 Gold Medal, Architecture Firm, and Twenty-five Year Award recipients as well as 18 additional award winners. The gallery was curated, planned, and designed by Melissa with the help of RAVA films and Pentagram.
