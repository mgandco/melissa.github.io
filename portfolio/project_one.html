Speak Up!
An advocacy and action event.

identity / logo

Debuting in July 2016, SpeakUp is three exciting, dynamic and inspiring days where architects will get the tools they need to advocate for their profession and community.

SpeakUp features legislative and political advocacy training to help architects raise their voice for architects at the federal, state and local levels.
